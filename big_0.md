# Big-O notation

* Keep fastest growing term in computation
* Drop constant factors in the computation formula

Space & time complexity

1. $$O(1)$$
2. $$O(log_2(n))$$
3. $$O(n)$$
4. $$O(n^2)$$
5. $$O(nlog_2(n))$$


To differentiate between O(log(n)) and O(n), increase n: 10 -> 100 -> 1000; Check if the increase in time is linear(O(log(n))) or quadratic(O(n))
