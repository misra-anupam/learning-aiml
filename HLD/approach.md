# Interview time allocation

1. Requirements clarification, scope limitations, metrics etc. (5 mins)
    - Features & non-functional requirements
2. Propose HLD and get buy in (15 mins)
    - HLD should satisfy feature requirements & their non-functional requirements(partcularly latency, scale & performance)
    - Do not deep-dive
    - Discuss data models & databases
3. Design deep-dive (20 mins)
    - Discuss trade-offs on non-functional requirements for each service/component
4. Wrap up (5 mins)
